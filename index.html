The act of converting concepts, answers, and instructions into binary machine code,
a language that computers can comprehend, is known as coding. Coding is the method used by
people to communicate with computers. Computers can obey a set of instructions made through
code. What a computer can and cannot do is governed by these instructions. Coding enables
developers to create programs, including websites and apps. Computer programmers may
instruct machines on how to better and more quickly process data.
