This is me and Osman Barrie's BlackJack game. How to play is when you hit you will recieve a card and then hit again.
once you hit again you'll most likely to hit stand once you hit stand the dealer will receieve 3 cards.
We change the rules a bit so if the game is not over you can decide if you want to hit or stand again.
these two buttons are for if you want the full size of the game & if you want a visual demonstration on how to play.
