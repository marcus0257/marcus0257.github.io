Blackjack Game

Unfortunately, we coded the game in 600 x 600, and it is not translating into GitHub, so bear with me. This is me and Osman Barrie's Blackjack game. Here's how to play:

  1. Click "Hit" to receive a card.
  2. Click "Hit" again if you want another card. You can choose to "Stand" when you're ready.
  3. Once you choose "Stand," the dealer will receive three cards.
  4. We've changed the rules a bit, so if the game is not over, you can decide if you want to hit or stand again.
